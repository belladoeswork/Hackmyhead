Synopsis

In a wacky Rhode Island town, a dysfunctional family of 6 strives to cope with everyday life as they are thrown from one crazy scenario to another.

Characters

The show usually revolves around the adventures of Peter Griffin. His wife Lois and him have three children: teenage daughter Meg, teenage son Chris, and son Stewie. Living with the family is Brian, the family dog.

Main cast

  • Seth MacFarlane as: Peter Griffin, Stewie Griffin, & Brian.
  • Alex Borstein as: Lois Griffin.
  • Seth Green as: Chris Griffin.
  • Mila Kunis as: Meg Griffin.
